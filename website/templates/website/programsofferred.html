Senior High School

Under the Enhanced Basic Education Act of 2013, senior high school (SHS) students must pick among four tracks. Upon enrollment in SHS, they may have the privilege of selecting a track that suits their interest and skills. Holy Child Central Colleges Inc. has laid a variety of options for upcoming Senior High School Students to enroll with.

Academic Track

This track appeals to those who have set their minds towards college education. Divided into degree-specific courses, the Academic track of HCCCI in senior high school aims to prepare students for more advanced college courses. Under this umbrella are four strands.

  • (GA) General Academics
  • (HUMMS) Humanities and Social Sciences
  • (STEM) Science Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics
  • (ABM) Accountancy, Business, and Management

Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) Track:

Holy Child Central Colleges Inc. prepares eligible students with subjects focused on job-ready skills. Besides, it offers practical knowledge with matching certificates to help students land their desired job after they graduate from SHS. HCCCI, in the same way, has provided partner industries ready to train and hire graduates of this track. Under the senior high school Technical-Vocational-Livelihood track are the following strands.

  • Computer System Servicing (NCII)
  • Computer Programming
  • Industrial Arts
  • Animation
  • Cookery
  • House Keeping
  • Wellness Massage
  • Food and Beverage Services
