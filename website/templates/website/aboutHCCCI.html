ABOUT HCCCI

Holy Child Central Colleges Inc. is a special place, filled with diverse, energetic, and talented people who are driven to grow and succeed. By studying here, you join an active community that advances the mission of the school in meaningful ways, inspires innovation and collaboration, and builds skills and expertise.

We offer a wide range of Strands (Senior High School) and variety of courses in college department with the support of our world-class academic facilities. We invite you to explore the many reasons to study for HCCCI:

Our insparing mission Here, we are committed to nurture and train students to be technologically-advanced individuals to help improve people’s lives in the community and the world, to contribute in the advancing research that makes a difference in the world, in fields as varied as Criminology, Education, Pharmacy, Information Technology and Business.

Meet amazing people. You have the opportunity to study with incredible talented educators and young people among them, top class athletes, gifted dancers, high achiever, innovators, and problem solvers.

Experience multicul-cultural opportunities. Get to experience diversified culture of HCCCI. We are rich in cultural heritage and the beauty of nature in surrounding localities. Here, students of different culture study and build relationship harmoniously.

Advance your education. Here, lifelong learning is encouraged. Take classes and have your basic education and higher education invested in HCCCI as we give you up-to-date lessons and meaningful learning experiences that you can use to become globally competitive individuals.

Explore our modern campus. Our campuses are equipped with audio visual materials, educational laboratories, updated libraries and CCTV cameras to secure the safety of all the students. Campus1 building is designed by leading architect to provide conducive teaching and learning space. Our campuses are also conveniently located near transportation along the national highway, restaurants, commercial stores, and school canteen that makes it accessible and feasible for all the needs of students.

School Seal

The Official Holy Child Central Colleges Inc. Seal

THE SHIELD

The shield encapsulates all the other elements of the logo to highlight the protection and security that HCCCI provides. As an educational institution, HCCCI aims to prepare its students for their future careers with marketable professional skills.

THE LAUREL WREATH

The laurel wreath is one of the highest symbols of honor dating back to ancient times. The wreath in the logo symbolizes HCCCI’s call for its students and alumni to be honorable, well disciplined, and responsible.

THE LIGHTED TORCH

The lighted torch represents the undying fire that fuels the love for knowledge intrinsic in HCCCI as an educational institution. It is behind the book as it provides the light and warmth in learning.

THE WORLD IN THE CHILDS HAND

The world held by the child represents that the children are the future. The child holding the world is a representation of a child changing the world. We help in making, shaping and achieving that change through education as we exist in this world.

THE COLOR

Khaki as the color symbolizes our culture as a Filipino; the logo used two dominant shades, namely, the light and dark shades. These two represent how both coexist despite their diversity. HCCCI underscores the need for harmony, integration and non-discrimination.

THE OPEN BOOK

The open book underscores HCCCI’s goal to provide high quality education for those that enters its portals. Every student and alumni are enjoined to become open vessels of learning; and radiate what they learn to their community.

THE CHILDS HAND

The hand represents the guiding and firm grasp of the Holy Child; the innocence of the Holy Child embodies the importance of spiritual growth in honing the future of HCCCI students.

THE ACADEMIC CAP OVER THE WORLD

The academic hat over the world represents the HCCCI’s world class standardized education. We provide nothing less than the above mentioned quality that makes our school give education with world class standard and capability of world competence. The HCCCI values, standards, and education can and will meet the competitive world with pride and honor. The HCCCI is globally competent.
